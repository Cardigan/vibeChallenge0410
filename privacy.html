Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What We Collect

We collect the phone number you provide and the reminder time you set. If you enter a custom message, that is also stored.

How We Use It

Your phone number is used solely to place automated reminder calls at the time you schedule. Verification codes are sent to confirm you own the phone number.

Third-Party Services

We use Twilio to make phone calls and send verification codes. Your phone number is shared with Twilio for this purpose. Please review Twilio's privacy policy for details on how they handle your data.

Data Storage

Your data is stored in a database for as long as your reminder is active. Once a reminder fires or is cancelled, you may request deletion of your data.

No Selling of Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

What We Don't Collect

We do not collect your name, email, browsing history, shoe size, astrological sign, or opinions on pineapple pizza. We genuinely only want your phone number, and even that feels like a big commitment.

Cookies

This app does not use cookies. We cannot, however, speak for your browser, your ISP, or that one browser extension you installed at 2 AM and forgot about.

Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will be just as surprised and upset as you are. Possibly more, because we'll also have to fix it.

Contact

For questions about this policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
